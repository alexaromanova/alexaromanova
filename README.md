I'm an M.Sc. student in **Analytics and Artificial Intelligence** at ESMT Berlin (Women in STEM & Analytics Scholarship recipient), with a B.Sc. in Business and Economics from WU Vienna.

My background sits at the intersection of **finance, data, and operations** — I spent a year as a finance intern at the **International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA)** in Vienna, where I streamlined payment authorization workflows and processed financial data 30% faster than average.
